Frequently Asked Questions

Do virtual appointments have the same quality as in-person appointments?

Yes! You will still be talking with an expert who is able to help meet your medical needs, whether you need prescribed medication or just need to ask a health related question. In fact, virtual meetings are more efficient due to little to no waiting time.

How can I schedule virtual appointments?

Simply click on our Meetings page, and choose which option you would like. We have appointments in three formats: video calls, phone calls, and text messages. Once you choose the option that works best for you, you can call or email us to set up an appointment.

You can click here to access the meetings page.

How has Eclipse Labs adjusted to the COVID-19 pandemic?

We take serious responsibility in keeping our employees safe. Our labs are now deep cleaned every day after use, and we always ensure everyone wears masks while maintaining a safe distance apart from each other. We hope to assist hospitals that are being overcrowded in treating patients, so that nobody has to be turned away.

Can I buy healthcare products online?

We have a Resources tab with many of our purchasable products. You can click on each product to see more details.

History


In Eclipse Labs' 20 year history, no customer has been left unsatsified. Eclipse Labs has regularly been left positive reveiws. The three founders took on the initiative to help people in need when they met at college. It is there, that they started Project Eclipse to help people in need after witnessing firsthand how many people needed healthcare. They continued Project Eclipse even when they graduated, and expanded Project Eclipse for their passion of helping people. Now, Eclipse Labs is a international company with over 250 established locations across the globe, helping people everywhere!

Locations
