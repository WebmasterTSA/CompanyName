At Eclipse Labs, an innovative telecommunications company, we dedicate our time and effort to ensure your well being. Experts at Eclipse Labs will make sure you, the patient, are seen and diagnosed under any circumstances with the exact precision and quality of any other face to face meeting.

Equal Treatment


"Our sole purpose lies in helping you, no matter who you are, live a healthier life."



When Eclipse Labs was founded, it was based on the idea of helping others, no matter their differences. To this day, we still value that ideal, and strive to treat everyone with the same importance and value in their health and as a person.



Prioritizing Safety


We take the pandemic very seriously, and we encourage others to do so as well. While many of our employees are working virtually, those who work at our labs must come in order to meet the healthcare needs of the public. We still prioritize our employees' safety. Along with daily cleaning, we require masks in our facilites. Employees stay a safe distance apart with minimal physical interaction with each other.


We sincerely thank all those who are risking their lives and working hard to provide for everybody during this stressful situation.



Service


At Eclipse Labs, we want to give back. We have had many successful charity events over the years, and we plan to do more as the company grows. Recently, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, we have volunteered to create, test, and distribute COVID-19 testing kits. We are aware of the rapid demand, and we would like to do as much as we can and join the effort of other healthcare providers.

History


In Eclipse Labs' 20 year history, no customer has been left unsatsified. Eclipse Labs has regularly been left positive reveiws. The three founders took on the initiative to help people in need when they met at college. It is there, that they started Project Eclipse to help people in need after witnessing firsthand how many people needed healthcare. They continued Project Eclipse even when they graduated, and expanded Project Eclipse for their passion of helping people. Now, Eclipse Labs is a international company with over 250 established locations across the globe, helping people everywhere!

Locations


Eclipse Labs is Headquartered in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania. With over 250 established locations across the globe, 25,000 employees at your service, 50,000 voluenteers worldwide, over 250 help programmes, Eclipse Labs is ready and avalible to help at any time, and at any locale.


Update: Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many of our locations have closed to the public in order to prevent the spread. We currently only allow authorized personell. Customers can still order products online.
